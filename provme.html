Company

Quacks Interatividade Digital

A Virtual Dressing Room that uses Game-Development Techniques(like Rigging and Skeletal Animation) to simulate real pieces of clothing in any picture.

The Project started with a outsourcing demand of a clothing store trying to create a virtual version of their clothing. we sketched out the initial idea and ended up transforming in a full-fledged virtual dressing room.

Our Major problems was finding a cheap way to recreate a infinite number of clothes while giving our possible clients the best quality. we applied some game-based tech like sprite rigging and skinning to bring this project to life.
