Wind Peaks

Duration

1 Months

Role

Level Designer, Game Programmer

Period

03/2019 - 04/2020

Company

Mental Lab

We wanted to create a game inspired on the popular series “Gravity Falls” with a pointer-click/Search game mechanic. We knew from the start that we wanted to create a underlying weird story that would be up to the players to interpret together with some welcoming cartoon art that would invite the player to explore the scenarios as much as looking for hidden objects.

I was in charge of the level design and some basic programming for peripheral mechanics in each level. although the main challenge seemed to be finding the perfect spot to hide every object, and it really was hard even more so when you took into account difficult scaling, the really hard stuff was finding a way to use this objects in a connected way between levels while also connecting them to the local story of that level in a way that we could guide the experience of the player without forcing them into a specific path.

In each level we also tried to create a few off-mechanic puzzles so that the player could take a mental rest of just looking for hidden objects and try his best and solving a riddle/puzzle.
