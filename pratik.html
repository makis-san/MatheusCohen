Pratik

Duration

5 Months

Role

Project Manager, Game Designer

Period

02/2019 - 07/2020

Company

Quacks Interatividade Digital

Creating of a Game-Based Physics lab that could be used by low-end devices on public schools while controlled by a in-class teacher.

The Project started with a simple game of 2 cannons trying to shoot each other, but instead of aiming the player had to use real physics to calculate the values of each shot. we quickly noticed that this pattern could be recreated for a array of topics and decided to expand on the ideia.

Upon the first tests, we noticed that game-based was the wrong approach for the project and decided to take a simulation approach to give better value for teachers and students alike.
