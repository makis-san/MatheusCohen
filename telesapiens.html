Telesapiens

Duration

8 Months

Role

Project Manager, Game Designer

Period

05/2019 - 01/2020

Company

Quacks Interatividade Digital

While working for Quacks, we have a big educational Project where we had to Design/Produce over 300 small educational games for Telesapiens(a local digital-editor).

The Project started with a simple game of 2 cannons trying to shoot each other, but instead of aiming the player had to use real physics to calculate the values of each shot. we quickly noticed that this pattern could be recreated for a array of topics and decided to expand on the ideia.

We started with trying to create a pipeline for a large-scale production and a few templates to speed up things as we created this pipeline. Alligning the client spectations with what we could deliver was also a big milestone, although it never became a problem, and proved crucial to customer success as we trailed a almost a year long road.

Resource allocation also proved a major point as we had to avoid work pending while we sometimes had incoming urgent demand on unexpected dates and eventual re-works as the client had some mind-changes at the last minute.
