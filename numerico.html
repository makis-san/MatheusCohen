Numerico

Duration

1 Month

Role

Game Designer, Game Progammer

Period

02/2019 - 03/2020

Company

Mental Lab

A quick project made from scratch of a small briefing. A Mathematical word hunting game. Our main challenges where finding how people interpreted equations when presented in different ways and how easy would be to find the logical combinations.

In this project i was in charge of Game Design, Game Programming and Level Design. We started with 2 challegends being if we should make procedural level design and if word hunting was indeed the best reference for the game. We ended up choosing to change a little and go for crosswords as a reference but noticed that the procedural level, although easy to make, would take a little too long for the scope of the project and decided to focus on small hand-made levels instead.

As a small scoped project, there was not that much pivoting through the project and luckily many of our initial assumptions turned out to be right during testing.

We started by testing how people interpreted basic formulas and worked quite alright, with a good mix of trial-and-error for more lazy players and quite a bit of math-play from people that were actually looking for a good mind-bend.
